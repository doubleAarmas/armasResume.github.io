More About Me

I started off working in human resources due to my background in customer service and hoping to better the environment for the employees I work alongside. For some time, everything went well and I rose through the ranks as I gained promotions and expanded my skill sets in HR.

After covid, an amazing opportunity at Amazon appeared before me, and I moved from my hometown of Miami, FL to Murfreesboro, TN. I spent the next 1.5 years working as an HR Partner in the largest Amazon warehouse in the state of TN! Mentoring the HR team on night shift, through several investigations and increasing our metrics, we slowly became the highest performing group on the HR team.

However, I wanted to do more. Given how the tech industry has been booming, I took a promotion to become a tech sourcing recruiter for consumer engagement at Amazon. Unfortunately, 6 months in, Amazon had their December 2022 layoffs but I felt this was an opportunity in disguise. After getting to know my candidates through several interviews I took an interest in becoming an SDE myself and will now be part of the February 2023 cohort at Brainstation here in Miami, FL.
